$2, 3 \implies 1$
## Пункт 2

$or d(a)=\infty,\ a^{m}=a^{n}$ НУО $m>n$
$$
a^{n}\cdot a^{m-n}=a^{n}\cdot e \implies a^{m-n}=e \implies m=n
$$

$$
\begin{gathered}
m=q_{m}\cdot k + r_{m}\\
n = q_{n}\cdot k+r_{n}
\end{gathered}\implies a^{m}=a^{q_{m}k+r_{m}}=(a^{k})^{q_{m}}\cdot a^{r_{m}}=a^{r_{m}}
