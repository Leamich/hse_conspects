$||h||=1$, $f:E\to \mathbb{R},\ E\subset \mathbb{R}^{n}$, $a \in \mathrm{Int}\,E$

$$
\frac{\delta f}{\delta h} (a):= \lim\limits_{ t \to 0,\ t \in \mathbb{R} } \frac{f(a+th)-f(a)}{t}
$$
